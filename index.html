About

Brianna Worldly

Owner / Operator

Brianna is a Long Island native with over 10 years of experience in pet care and pet shelters. She started NAG when the shelter (she was previously employed by) closed down. She has turned her love and passion for animals in to a full time career and shows no signs of slowing down.
